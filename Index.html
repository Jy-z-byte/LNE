Night Economy Trends in London

 

London’s nightlife is booming! Over the years, more and more nightspots have popped up across the city. The interactive 3D map on the right brings this growth to life.

 

→ Slide through time to watch different boroughs evolve. Hover over an area to see how many venues were buzzing that year!

Crime Trends in London

 

As London’s nightlife thrives, crime rates have also been on the rise in some areas. Busy nights can bring big crowds—but also potential safety concerns.

 

→ Click on a borough to track its crime trends over time in the chart on the right.

London’s nightlife is buzzing—but is it fair and safe for everyone?

 

This project dives into the city’s night economy, exploring how busy nighttime spots connect with local wealth and accessibility. We’re also looking at safety—think crime rates, police presence, and surveillance coverage—to see what’s really happening after dark.

 

With data-driven insights and interactive tools, we’re shining a light on both the risks and rewards of London’s nightlife. Let’s make the city’s nights safer, fairer, and more vibrant for all!

A project by NUS AY24/25 DEP5118 Group 6, guided by Assistant Prof. Ahn Chaewon.

Data from London Datastore, TfL, OSM, Met Police.
